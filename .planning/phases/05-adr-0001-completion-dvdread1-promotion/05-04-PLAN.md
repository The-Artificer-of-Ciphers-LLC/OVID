<objective>
Fix the genuine IDENT-03 gap in ARM's auto-register path: `arm/identify_ovid.py::submit_to_ovid()` currently sends only the bare primary fingerprint with zero `fingerprint_aliases`, even though `identify_dvd()`/`identify_bd()` may have computed additional identity strings locally. Add `fingerprint_disc_with_identity()` to extract both the primary fingerprint and its alias list from the already-parsed `Disc`/`BDDisc` object, extend `submit_to_ovid()` to accept and conditionally include `fingerprint_aliases`, and thread the aliases through `arm/identify.py`'s miss-fallback registration call.

Purpose: "the client submits every known Disc Identity string" (IDENT-03) must hold for EVERY submission path, not just the interactive CLI wizard — ARM's automated auto-register-on-miss path is the other real-world submission path, and today it silently drops every alias.
Output: `fingerprint_disc_with_identity()` and an extended `submit_to_ovid()` in `identify_ovid.py`; wired-through alias threading in `identify.py`; a new `arm/tests/` package with full coverage; a new CI job running it.
</objective>
